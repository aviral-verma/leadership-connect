CUSTOMER LIFE CYCLE - LIFE CYCLE BASED SALES APPROACH

  • During the life cycle of the FD, we try to build trustful relationship with the customers by providing quality services.
  • It is important to build good relationship with the customers because once the FD is matured, we do not have any contact with the customers unlike banks who frequently tries to cross sale other products because the customer has a current/saving bank account so it is very easy for them to be in constant touch with the customers.
  • Quality services ensure that a trustful relationship builds between us and the customers so that they come back to us again in future when they have some money to invest or even renew the FD in case they do want to use the money.

Product - Loan Against Fixed Deposits

  • BFL provides loan against Fixed Deposits of upto 75% of the principal deposit
  • The interest rate for such loans is 2% more than that offered on deposit
  • BFL has capped the loan at 75% of deposit as per the Circular from RBI
  • Banks provide loan against Fixed deposits of upto 90% of the principal deposit because banks have liquidity from Current and Savings accounts also apart from the term deposits.
  • The 75% cap ensures that there is no ALM (Asset Liability Mismatch).

Market Disruption

  • Fixed Deposit is a vanilla product, regulated by RBI so there is very little scope to customize the product.
  • So, we can only customize our services by providing quality services, creating customer convenience and reducing our various costs by adopting digitalization such as chatbots, self servicing platforms, digital payments etc.
  • Our 3in1 Super App is one of the steps taken to disrupt the market.
